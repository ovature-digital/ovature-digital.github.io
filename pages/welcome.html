Welcome Pack

Two A6 double-sided flyer designs for Vibe’s 2017 Welcome Pack.

With a large influx of new visitors each week, Vibe hands each newcomer a small gift to welcome them to the family. James Benjamin and Ps. Amos Ngoi wanted to revamp the design of the current 'Welcome Pack' with updated cultural pillars and information accompanied by snacks and collateral.
